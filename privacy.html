Privacy Policy

This policy covers caleeacu.com and research.caleeacu.com.

Effective date: July 5, 2026

Who We Are

CALee Acupuncture is an acupuncture clinic located at 6881 Stanton Ave, Ste F, Buena Park, CA 90621. This privacy policy describes how our websites — caleeacu.com and research.caleeacu.com (together, the "Sites") — handle information about visitors.

Information We Collect

The Sites do not contain forms and do not ask you to enter personal information. We collect only the following:

  • Usage data (automatic). We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the Sites. This collects information such as the pages you visit, your approximate location (city level), device and browser type, and an anonymized identifier stored in a cookie. We do not use this data to identify you personally.
  • Information you choose to send us. If you contact us by text message, phone, or email using the links on the Sites, that communication happens through your own phone or email app. We receive only what you choose to send, and we use it solely to respond to you and schedule care.

Online Booking

Appointment booking is provided by Square. When you book, any information you enter is collected and processed by Square under its own privacy policy, available at squareup.com/legal/privacy.

Medical Information

The Sites do not collect medical or health information. Any health information you share with us as a patient is collected in the clinic and handled in accordance with applicable healthcare privacy laws, separately from the Sites.

Third-Party Services

The Sites use the following third-party services, each governed by its own privacy policy:

  • Google Analytics — website usage measurement
  • Google Fonts — typeface delivery
  • Square — online appointment booking
  • Google Blogger — blog articles linked from research.caleeacu.com

Cookies and Your Choices

The only cookies set by the Sites are Google Analytics cookies. You can decline them by adjusting your browser's cookie settings, or by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on. The Sites remain fully usable without cookies.

Do Not Track

Some browsers offer a "Do Not Track" (DNT) signal. Because no common industry standard for responding to DNT signals has been adopted, the Sites do not currently respond to them. Our data practices remain as described in this policy regardless of DNT settings.

We Do Not Sell Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade any visitor information, and we do not share it with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

Children

The Sites are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children through the Sites.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will post the updated version on this page with a new effective date.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to caleeacu@gmail.com or by mail to CALee Acupuncture, 6881 Stanton Ave, Ste F, Buena Park, CA 90621.
